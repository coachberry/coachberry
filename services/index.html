Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located? Do you coach remotely? +
I'm based in Nashville, TN and coach primarily in the Nashville area. I also offer video analysis and remote coaching sessions for players anywhere in the country.
What age groups do you work with? +
I work with players from youth hockey all the way through university level. I tailor my coaching approach to the age and experience level of each player.
Do you only coach goalies? +
Goaltending is my specialty through 3D Goaltending, but I coach all positions through 3D Hockey LLC. I offer skills coaching, team coaching, and development programs for skaters at all levels.
How do I get pricing information? +
Pricing depends on the type of service, frequency, and location. Create a free account and reach out through your member dashboard — I'll get back to you quickly with details.
What is the 3D Goalie Tool? +
The 3D Goalie Tool is a development platform I built for my goalie coaching clients. Goalies can log goals, saves, and submit pre-session intake forms. I review everything and add coach notes, plus the system generates AI-powered development insights. Access is currently free for my active goalie clients.
