About Coach Berry

Hockey Coach & Goaltender Specialist

With 30+ years of playing experience across all positions and 8+ years of dedicated coaching, I'm passionate about developing players both on and off the ice through open communication and innovative training techniques.

Currently, I'm the Owner/Lead Coach of 3D Hockey LLC, Head Coach of the U18 Nashvillains Summer Team, and Assistant Coach at Page-Summit High School. I've coached at every level from youth to university, including Stevenson University ACHA D2 & D3 and Johns Hopkins University.

My coaching philosophy centers on individualized programs tailored to each player's unique needs and challenges, combined with high-quality video analysis and educational content.

Certifications & Credentials
