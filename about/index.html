Hockey Coach & Goaltender Specialist

With over 30 years of playing experience across all positions and 8+ years of dedicated coaching, I'm passionate about developing players both on and off the ice. I'm the Owner and Lead Coach of 3D Hockey LLC and 3D Goaltending, based in Nashville, TN.

My coaching philosophy is built on the belief that development happens on and off the ice. I work with players to build technical skills, mental toughness, and the character traits that make great players and great people.
